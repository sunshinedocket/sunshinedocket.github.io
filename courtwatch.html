Why this matters now. The income tax on earners above $1 million (SB 6346) is being challenged in Klickitat County Superior Court. The case will reach this court. Two of the five seats on the ballot are held by Ferguson appointees. Two more are open because sitting justices aged out or chose not to run. The composition of the court that hears the tax case will be decided by voters this November.
