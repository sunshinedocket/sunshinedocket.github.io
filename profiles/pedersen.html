Sen. Jamie Pedersen

D-43 | Senate Majority Leader | EVP & General Counsel, McKinstry Co.
Conflict

Summary

Pedersen is the Executive Vice President and General Counsel of McKinstry Co., a construction and energy services company that received $29.1 million in state agency payments in calendar year 2025. He chairs the Senate Ways & Means Committee, which controls the operating budgets of the agencies paying his employer. He also sits on the Legislative Ethics Board that would investigate any conflict complaint against him.

His McKinstry salary jumped from $200K-$500K (CY2024) to $500K-$750K (CY2025). His employer promoted him to Executive Vice President while he continued to chair the committee funding their $29 million in state contracts.
